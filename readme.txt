A cool, fully customizable widget to show off your Google My Business Reviews average rating in stars, thumbs or squares.

== Description ==

A cool widget, developed by [Daan van den Bergh](https://www.daanvandenbergh.nl "My Wordpress & Joomla Development-blog") from [Semster](https://www.semster.nl "Semster: Web Development en Internet Marketing") to show off your Google My Business Reviews rating in stars, thumbs or squares. It comes with plenty of customizations to easily adjust the lay-out of the widget to fit in with your site. Also, the included CSS Stylesheet only includes the necessary styling for correct display of the widget. So, no hard-coded fonts, etc.!

Features:
---------
- Insert link to "All Google Reviews"-page;
- Fully translatable and adjustable text;
- Choice between stars, thumbs and squares;
- Adjustable rating scale;
- Adjustable alignment of icons and text;
- Completely free!

This plug-in is currently in beta-stage. Which means that bugs are likely to occur.
Known bugs:
-----------
 	No known bugs ATM.

Features to be added in the near future:
----------------------------------------
	Display Google Reviews;
	Display Google My Business profile picture;
	Disable stylesheet;
	Run Google API-request thru wp_cron, instead of at page request;
	And more...

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the '/wp-content/plugins/google-my-business-reviews/' directory, or install directly from the Wordpress plugins repository (recommended).
2. Activate the plugin, through the 'Plugins'-screen in Wordpress.
3. Activate the widget by dragging it into your widget area.
4. Adjust the settings according to your wishes.
	If you need help finding your LRD, please visit [this page](http://www.uksbd.co.uk/how-to-find-your-lrd-number/ "Find your LRD").
	Visit [this page](https://developers.google.com/places/place-id "Find your Google Places ID") to find your Google Places ID.
	To obtain your API-key, follow the steps on [this page](https://developers.google.com/places/web-service/get-api-key "Obtain your API-key").
